About Our Heart Disease Prediction Tool

Welcome to our Heart Disease Prediction Tool!

What is This?

This web application is designed to help you predict the likelihood of heart disease based on a set of essential health attributes. We've built this tool using a comprehensive dataset that contains information about various individuals and their heart health.

The Dataset

Our predictions are powered by a dataset that includes:

  • Age: The age of the individual.
  • Sex: Whether the person is male or female.
  • Chest Pain Type: The type of chest pain experienced.
  • Resting Blood Pressure: Blood pressure at rest.
  • Cholesterol: Cholesterol levels.
  • Fasting Blood Sugar: Whether fasting blood sugar is high.
  • Resting Electrocardiographic Results: ECG results at rest.
  • Maximum Heart Rate Achieved: The highest heart rate during a test.
  • Exercise-Induced Angina: Presence of angina during exercise.
  • ST Depression Induced by Exercise: ST segment depression during exercise.
  • Slope: Slope of the peak exercise ST segment.
  • Number of Major Vessels: Count of major blood vessels.
  • Thalassemia: A blood disorder type.

How It Works

Our predictive model uses machine learning techniques to analyze these attributes and make a prediction about heart disease. We've trained the model using historical data to ensure accurate results.

Interpretation of Results

Prediction Outcome: A prediction of 0 indicates a lower likelihood of heart disease, while a prediction of 1 suggests a higher likelihood.
Accuracy: Our model strives for accuracy, but remember, it's a prediction, not a definitive diagnosis. Consult a healthcare professional for a complete evaluation.

Why Use Our Tool?

  • Early Detection: Detect potential heart issues early for timely intervention.
  • Risk Assessment: Understand your risk factors and make informed health decisions.
  • Easy to Use: Our user-friendly interface makes it simple to get your prediction.
